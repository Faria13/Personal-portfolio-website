About Me

A few words about me as a person

Hello! Welcome to my website. I would like to share my world with you. My name is Faria Mush Sultana from Bangladesh. I am a student of Computer Science Engineering at Noakhali Science &Technology University. I was a member of Prothom Alo Bondhushova club which is working for Community services. I had been working for two years in this club. We had organized two national events where at least 1500 young children have participated. It was a great experience for me. Now I am a member of Luminary club which is working for penniless young children where they are indoctrinated. I love to read a book and feel great to spend time with children.

I enjoy taking complex problems and turning them into simple and beautiful interface designs. I also love the logic and structure of coding and always strive to write elegant and efficient code. whether it be HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, Jquery or PHP. My passion is all about creating real elegant looking websites. Always keeping it clean and simple with that added functionally of user interaction you would like to see. I’m constantly learning web technologies and other design related topics, currently playing around with PHP and PHP Framework.
